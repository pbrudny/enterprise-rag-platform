Acme Corp 2026 Organizational Restructuring Plan (Restricted, Executive Only)

This document outlines the planned reorganization of Acme Corp's engineering division, effective Q1 2026.

The Platform and Data teams will be merged into a single Infrastructure organization reporting to the incoming VP of Engineering.

Approximately 8 percent of roles in the Infrastructure organization will be eliminated as part of this consolidation, primarily in duplicated middle-management positions.

This plan has not been communicated to the affected teams and must remain strictly confidential to executives until the formal announcement.
