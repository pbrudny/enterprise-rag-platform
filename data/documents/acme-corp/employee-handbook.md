Acme Corp Employee Handbook

Welcome to Acme Corp. This handbook covers general policies applicable to all employees.

Working hours are 9am to 5pm local time, Monday through Friday, with flexible start times between 8am and 10am agreed with your manager.

All employees are entitled to 25 days of paid annual leave per calendar year, plus public holidays observed in their home country.

Expense reports must be submitted within 30 days of the expense being incurred, via the internal Concur system, with receipts attached for any single item over 25 EUR.

New employees receive a laptop, a company badge, and access to the internal wiki during their first week. IT onboarding is handled by the Acme IT helpdesk.
