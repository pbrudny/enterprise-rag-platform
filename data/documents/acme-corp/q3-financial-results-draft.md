Acme Corp Q3 Financial Results (Draft, Confidential)

This draft summarizes Acme Corp's unaudited Q3 financial performance for internal review by the finance team ahead of the public earnings release.

Revenue for Q3 came in at 142 million EUR, up 9 percent quarter over quarter, driven primarily by the EMEA enterprise segment.

Gross margin improved to 61 percent, compared to 58 percent in Q2, due to renegotiated cloud infrastructure contracts.

Operating expenses increased by 4 percent, mostly attributable to headcount growth in the go-to-market organization.

This document must not be shared outside the finance team until the public earnings call scheduled for next month.
