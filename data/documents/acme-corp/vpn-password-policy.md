Acme Corp VPN and Remote Access Password Policy

All employees connecting to Acme Corp's internal network via VPN must use a password that is rotated every 90 days.

Passwords must be at least 14 characters long and must include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one symbol. Reuse of any of the last 10 passwords is not permitted.

VPN sessions are automatically terminated after 12 hours of continuous connection, and after 30 minutes of inactivity. Multi-factor authentication is required for all VPN logins using the Acme Authenticator app.

Employees who suspect their VPN credentials have been compromised must contact the Acme Security Operations Center immediately and rotate their password within one hour.
