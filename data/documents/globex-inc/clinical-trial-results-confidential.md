Globex Inc Clinical Trial Results Summary (Confidential)

This document summarizes preliminary results from the Phase II clinical trial of Globex Inc's remote patient monitoring platform, for internal review by the clinical and product teams.

The trial enrolled 340 patients across 12 sites, with a primary endpoint of reduction in unplanned hospital readmissions within 90 days.

Preliminary results show a 22 percent reduction in readmissions in the intervention group compared to the control group, which is statistically significant at p less than 0.01.

These results are preliminary and unaudited. They must not be shared externally or with regulatory bodies until the full data package has been reviewed by the clinical team.
