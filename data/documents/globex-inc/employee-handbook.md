Globex Inc Employee Handbook

Welcome to Globex Inc. This handbook covers general policies applicable to all employees across our healthcare technology division.

Standard working hours are 8am to 4pm local time, with on-call rotations for clinical systems support staff.

Employees receive 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year, plus applicable US federal holidays.

All employees handling patient-related systems must complete HIPAA compliance training within their first 30 days of employment and annually thereafter.

New employees are issued a company laptop and badge during orientation, and must complete security awareness training before receiving system access.
