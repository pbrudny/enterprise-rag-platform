Globex Inc Patient Data Handling Policy

All patient data processed by Globex Inc systems must be encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher.

Access to patient records is restricted to employees with an active clinical-systems assignment, and every access event is logged with the employee ID, patient record ID, and timestamp.

Patient data must not be exported to personal devices, printed, or transmitted via unencrypted email under any circumstances.

Any suspected unauthorized access to patient data must be reported to the Globex Privacy Office within one hour of discovery, per HIPAA breach notification requirements.
