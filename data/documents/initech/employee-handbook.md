Initech Employee Handbook

Welcome to Initech. This handbook covers general policies applicable to all employees across our Asia-Pacific operations.

Core working hours are 9am to 6pm local time, with a one-hour lunch break, Monday through Friday.

Employees receive 18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year, plus public holidays observed in their local jurisdiction.

New employees receive a laptop, an access badge, and complete a one-week onboarding program covering company systems and code-of-conduct training.
