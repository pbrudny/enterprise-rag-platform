Initech VPN and Remote Access Policy

All Initech employees connecting to the corporate network remotely must use the Initech VPN client with a password rotated every 60 days.

Passwords must be at least 12 characters long, containing a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Password history checks prevent reuse of the last 6 passwords.

VPN connections time out after 8 hours of continuous use and after 15 minutes of inactivity. Two-factor authentication via SMS or the Initech Authenticator app is mandatory for all remote access.

Any employee who believes their remote access credentials have been exposed must notify the Initech IT Security team immediately and reset their password without delay.
